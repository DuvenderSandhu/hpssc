WELCOME TO HIMACHAL PRADESH STAFF SELECTION COMMISSION

The Government of Himachal Pradesh in exercise of the powers conferred under article 162 read with proviso of Article 309 of the Constitution of India constituted the H.P. Staff Selection Commission with its headquarters at Hamirpur vide notification No. Per (AP.B) B (1)-1/98 dated 6th October 1998. The commission has been entrusted with the responsibility of making recommendations to all class-III services/posts including such posts on contract basis under the HP Govt. Besides this, the commission has also to make recommendations to all such class-III posts of the state public sector Undertakings, Boards, Corporations, Universities and Local Bodies, etc., as may be entrusted to the Board by such Boards/ Corporations/ Universities and Local Bodies etc. The commission receives various requisitions of TGT, SI, JE's, Clerk, Junior Office Assistant, Senior Scale Stenographer, Junior Scale Stenographer, Steno Typist, Computer Operator and Data Operator from different departments or state public sector Undertakings, Boards, Corporations, Universities and Local Bodies.

About the Department

The Govt. of Himachal Pradesh in exercise of the powers conferred under Article 162 read with proviso to the Article 309 of the Constitution of India constituted HP Subordinate Services Selection Board with its headquarters at Hamirpur vide Notification No. Per(AP.B)B(1)-/98 dated 6th Oct.1998. Now its nomenclature has been changed to HP Staff Selection Commission, Hamirpur vide Govt. notification No. Per(AP.B)B(1)-1/98 part dated 10-5-2016. The Commission has been entrusted with the responsibility of making recommendations to all Class-III services/posts including posts on contract basis under the HP govt. Besides this, the Commission has also to make recommendations to all such class-III posts of the State Public Sector Undertakings, Boards, Corporations, Universities and Local Bodies etc. as may be entrusted to the Commission by such Boards, Corporations, Universities and Local Bodies etc. This Commission has been established with the purpose of recruiting capable, competent, highly skilled individuals by subjecting them to the screening tests/skill tests wherever required followed by Evaluation as per government notification issued vide No. Per (AP.B) B (15)-5/2014 dated 17-4-2017.
The main endeavor of the Commission is to ensure that recruitment is done on merit in impartial and objective manner so that competent and capable candidates are recommended to the requisitioning authorities.

Member

Mission/vision

  • The commission has been established with the purpose of recruiting capable competent, skilled individuals by conducting written tests, professional tests (Typing, Short Hand etc.) and evaluation interviews as per the requirement and suitability.
  • The commission is committed to develop selection and recruitment procedures that conform to the highest standards in testing and ensure selection by fair means of the most competent, capable and skilled individuals for user department.
  • The commission continuously endeavors to ensure that the recruitment is done purely on merit in impartial and objective manner on time.
